Conflict of Interest

Under the Code of Ethics and Business Conduct Policy, employees must disclose potential or actual conflicts of interests before taking any action that may be seen as impacting their ability to make an impartial decision in Maxeon’s best interest. Declarations should be made using the Conflict of Interest Disclosure Form.

Gifts and Entertainment

Gifts and Entertainment, if given or received improperly, may create a risk or appearance of corruption, a conflict of interest, or improperly influence business decisions. Please refer to this policy for more details.

Based on the is policy, if you are required to disclose and seek pre-approval, please use this form: Gifts and Entertainment Form.

Third-Party Engagement

Under the Global Anti-Corruption Policy, all Third-Party engagement involving, Government Officials, as defined in this Policy, will need to be assessed for potential bribery risks prior to engagement.

If your proposed Third-Party engagement is for a new vendor, this risk is addressed as part of the new vendor onboarding exercise. Please click below for guidance on onboarding new vendors: New Vendor Onboarding Process
However, if your proposed Third-Party engagement is other than for a new vendor, please submit the Due Diligence Form.

Reporting Violations

Any conduct or transaction, no matter how insignificant, that Maxeon Personnel and Maxeon Entities know or reasonably believe might violate the Policy or applicable law must be reported promptly to the Company’s Chief Legal Officer and/or to the Company’s Compliance & Ethics Helpline, which may be accessed via country-specific toll-free phone numbers or web-based reports, the details of which are listed on https://maxeon.ethicspoint.com.

The Compliance & Ethics Helpline is available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week online or by phone to answer your questions on business conduct issues, policies, regulations and compliance with legal requirements. It also allows you to report known or potential violations of the law or our Code to Maxeon. When making a report, the more information you can provide, the easier it will be for Maxeon to investigate and appropriately respond to your report. Reports may be made in multiple languages. An independent third party administers the Helpline, including web-based reports. Where permitted, you may report anonymously. Caller ID is never used and there will be no effort to trace your call.
