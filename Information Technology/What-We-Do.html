Oracle Enterprise Business Applications                 

Oracle Enterprise Business Applications, also known as Oracle EBS, is an integrated set of business applications for automating enterprise resource planning (ERP) and supply chain management (SCM) & financials (FIN) processes within organizations. The Oracle E-Business Suite applications run on top of the flagship Oracle Database software.Oracle EBS Suite is designed to help users manage global businesses, improve decision making, reduce costs and increase corporate performance. EBS contains application modules that support different aspects of CRM, ERP and SCM operations, including customer service management, financial management, human capital management, project portfolio management, procurement, and value chain planning and execution. Oracle also has pre-integrated its business intelligence applications with EBS to provide EBS users with BI and analytics capabilities.

Enterprise PMO & Governance              

The IT Project Management Office and Governance provides strategic and operational support, monitoring, and project management guidance for all IT projects from project ideation until closure.​ For the portfolio, program, and project management side, it provides key functions on project status monitoring and reporting, project delivery and execution, project risk and compliance, to determine project health and alignment with business strategies and adherence to the IT Project Management and Software Development Lifecycle Framework.​ With regards to the IT governance aspect, the team ensures that at different functional levels there is transparency with stakeholders, diligence in the use of project resources and finances, and proper planning of project demand for both the IT projects and technology roadmaps.
